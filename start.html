What is this?

The Thoughtful Millennial is the podcast where we reflect on our rapidly evolving attitudes on everything from mental health to how to become a more sustainable human.

We hope to explore topics that are important to discuss about so we can questions our perceptions and explore other perspectives.

Who are we?

Halima is best described as an all-round problem-solver with a background in Engineering and Sustainable Development. She applies human-centred design and data-driven innovation in her everyday work. Outside of work has been involved in various international social impact programs centred around the UN Sustainable Development goals.

John is a Bolivian-born social entrepreneur and software developer. He co-founded an e-learning school platform which launched in Europe in 2021, as well as organising several social change programmes in Bolivia/Latin America. Most recently John has published an organization shining a light on mental health awareness and has built on the success of this by creating an online social media network, talking out to combat everyday gender inequality experienced in society.
